About ACM YCCE

The ACM YCCE Student Chapter is a vibrant community of computing enthusiasts at your college YCCE. We are dedicated to fostering professional growth, technical knowledge sharing, and building a collaborative network among students passionate about computing and information technology. Through workshops, seminars, hackathons, and community projects, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands.
